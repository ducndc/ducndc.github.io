Overview

History

I have studied computer engineering at Hanoi University of Science and Technology since September 2017. And then, I participated in research at EmNetLab at C9-411 of the Institute of Electronics and Telecommunications in June 2019. In the Lab, I researched IoT systems, developed UAV (Unmanned Aerial Vehicle), and implemented several projects about embedded systems. From September 2022, I have worked in the Transport Network Technologies Center of Viettel High Technology Industries Corporation(VHT), Viettel Group with an embedded software engineer. In this time, I researched and developed the firmware of network devices.
